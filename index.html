Employment History

In 2010 right after graduation from University my greatest adventure began. Moving to China to co-find a trading company and building a successful enterprise from the ground up was one of the most valuable experiences of my life. Because of our low startup capital, my partners and I had to confront many problems ourselves. As a result I gained new knowledge and skills in the fields of export, logistics, taxes and learned how to develop and maintain relationships with suppliers and customers all over the world. At the same time I was studying Chinese at the local University and working for Hong Kong branch of Kenfair Exhibition company.

In 2012 I co-found an agency for foreign models and other performers in China and Hong Kong. Mentioned company allowed me to learn how to manage human resources and organize events. It was in China where I received a chance to appear in my first commercial and to start working as a model and actor. Since that time I made it into short films, commercials, few TV shows and most important theater plays.
